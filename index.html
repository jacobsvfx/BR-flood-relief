BRFloodRelief
A community solution to flooding in Brazil

Collaborating with local government officials and international health organizations has given us the amazing opportunity to assist the citizens of Rio Grande do Sul with the ongoing bacterial spread caused by major flooding.

Almost half a million people have already lost their homes, being forced to leave and find shelter elsewhere. We want to stop these floods from hurting any more people than they already have, as much as possible. Consider scrolling through our website to find out more about what YOU can do to help.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I explore this website in Portuguese?

Clicking the "Translate" button in the menu will convert all text to Portuguese!

What made you want to start this inivitative?

When studying Antimicrobial Resistance in the world, we found that Leptospira spread through flood water in Brazil were becoming increasingly resistant. To help aid local residents of Rio Grande do Sul, I created this website.

How can I get involved with flood relief efforts?

You can get involved by volunteering, donating, or spreading awareness through social media. Check our homepage for links and resources to help support those affected by the floods.

Where can I find updates about the flood situation in Brazil?

We regularly update our website with the latest news and resources. You can also follow our blog or subscribe to our newsletter for real-time updates and important information.

How will local Brazilians know about this website?

We are working to spread this website and flood awareness through social media posts and contacting local officials.
